The Last Week

It is possible that we will need Monday as a continuation of the previous week. If that is the case, the report will be due Friday.

  • Weekend
    Prototype report. Submit a 3+ page report detailing your prototype. Your report should have a short Background, where you explain your design idea. It should be followed by a section titled Prototype Design, where you describe your prototype, using circuit diagrams and photos as necessary.
  • Monday
    KCAT, followed by work time.
  • Wednesday
    Work time.
  • Friday
    Second Prototype Report. Submit a 3+ page report Begin with a brief Introduction, where you recap your design and prototype. Then, a Design Evolution section, where you describe what next steps you believe are necessary to transform your prototype into a working, installed unit. Finally, you should include both your Team Evaluation and Self Reflection, which I will provide you with additional information about.

Your final reports are due by or before the final exam slot.

Post-Turkey Break

  • Weekend
    Turkey Weekend.

  • Monday
    Class canceled.

  • Wednesday
    Regroup. You want to accomplish three things this week:

    1. Lay out circuit diagram (neatly!) for a prototype control circuit.
    2. “Board” the circuit. This is a prototyping step, where you lay out everything in an exploded form, so as to clearly document and test your design.
    3. Code the circuit. You should have software running on your board that demonstrates how the full circuit works.

  • Friday
    Lecture: What You’ve Learned, What Comes Next, followed by work time.
